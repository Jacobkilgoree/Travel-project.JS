Village Pointe is an open-air shopping, dining, and entertainment destination in the heart of Omaha. From Scheels, Apple, Coach, Kendra Scott, and lululemon to Kona Grill and Firebirds Wood Fired Grill, Village Pointe is Omaha’s destination for great shopping and dining. Laugh it up at The Funny Bone Comedy Club, or catch a new flick at Marcus Theatres—Village Pointe has it all.
